What about Me?

My name is Anastasia Webb, I am an 11th grade student studying to become a software developer through full stack development. I attend Clara Barton High School and I go to the Brooklyn Steam Center. I've been practicing my own side projects of making things using code here and there.

I have many interests, Here are the top.

I have many interests when it comes to things outside of working. These fall under the category of baking, drawing and at the side, and lastly playing guitar. These interests come from many inspirations that i've gotten from online.

My first interest, when it comes to baking comes from dessert shops. I've passed many shops in manhattan the few times I go. I see the way people decorate cakes and make them look pretty. Putting fruits along the top, Stylizing whipped cream, all of those were big inspirations that told me I should bake one day. I chose to go from basic cake mix boxes into cake mixes all from scratch.

My second interest is art. I've been doing so since about elementary level, drawing everyday. I enjoyed being able to express things coming from my mind. As I grew older, it became more involved with me as a person. Art was an escape in a way of speaking. I drew to get things out my head that bothered me, I drew as gifts for people, soon enough, I had even moved onto painting.
